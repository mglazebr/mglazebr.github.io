Introduction

New Wave

This is a magazine about women's streetwear fashion. This magazine will highlight the current trends in streetwear fashion across the world. It will include articles on trends, where to buy the looks, current brands that are setting trends, and celebrities who are rocking the latest styles out in public. This will be a graphic heavy magazine to show off as many looks and styles as possible.
